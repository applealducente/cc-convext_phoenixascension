Step 3: Qualify the Objection

Note to agent: if the customer says no to the membership, explore "why" to better match their needs.

Totally fair. Just so I can better understand, is it more about the cost, because there's a commitment, or do you just feel like you don't need regular cleanings right now?

Step 4: Negotiate

• If declining because of MF: reduce to $54, then to $49 if necessary.
• If declining because of MCT duration: offer to shorten.
• If both: offer reducing both.
• If the response has nothing to do with cost/commitment: proceed to booking the one-time clean.

If the customer compared the FC ETF and the One-Time rate: That's why the FC membership offers better value overall. It's designed for ongoing service, but even if your plans change unexpectedly, the early termination fee is a flat rate of $35/hr based on the hours of your first cleaning. It's still lower than the ($X) one-time cleaning rate, so you're getting the best rate either way.
